Redwoods are being subjected to the effects of climate change: temperature increases, less coastal fog, reductions in snowpacks.
These forests store more carbon than any other forest ecosystem, and they support communities of life found nowhere else on Earth.
With a little help from us the Redwood forest can recover. It will take care of itself and us for millions of years to come.
