Home Playground

This project centers around mistreatment of pregnant women in prison and the particularly shocking story of Siwatu-Salama Ra that exemplifies it.

Objectives

The objectives of this project are to describe some of the hardships endured by pregnant incarcerated women and to prompt the user to reach out to their legislator to protest it.

Interactions

Each image and text, except the center, lead to other pages that reveal other parts of Ra's story or outside resources around the issue.

Sources

This information is from new sources and social media outlets that used the #freesiwatu and #unshacklepregnancy tags.
